Journal

This journal records the site week by week, from first sketches and workshop exercises to layout decisions, styling tests, and final refinements.

Week 1 / Structure

I began by documenting workshop exercises and sketching possible layouts. My focus was on understanding how HTML creates order, and how a page can be broken into sections, image blocks, and navigation systems.

Week 2 / Layout

During the second week I tested grids, spacing, and visual hierarchy. I started translating paper wireframes into browser-based compositions, comparing different ways to balance text and image.

Week 3 / Interaction

The third week expanded the project through motion and responsiveness. I experimented with hover states, transitions, and early interactive ideas, while continuing to collect screenshots and process documentation.

Week 4 / Refinement

In the final stage I unified the site through a shared palette, stronger typography, and more consistent page structures. The workbook became not just a record of exercises, but a coherent portfolio of development.
