P5.JS

This interactive study turns the language of the sketch into motion: drifting stars, directional lines, and a floating field that stretches across the whole page. The composition responds to movement while keeping the quiet rhythm of the hand-drawn sitemap.

Interaction Notes

The sketch is based on the star composition from my sitemap. Outlined forms drift slowly through a dark field, while directional strokes suggest movement, repetition, and spatial flow.

  • Move the mouse to shift the speed and tension of the composition
  • Stars drift subtly across the canvas over time
  • Click to reshuffle the star positions
