Research

Reference Sites

  • Marta Di Francesco — restrained typography and quiet hierarchy
  • Spectra Eye Clinic — modular grid, contrast, and image-led layout
  • Palette study — dark neutrals, soft contrast, and warm highlights

Notes

My research focused on how editorial restraint can still feel expressive. I was interested in layouts that leave space for images to breathe, while using typography and repetition to create rhythm.

I also looked at how contrast works emotionally: dark surfaces can make photography feel more luminous, and muted neutrals can keep the interface calm rather than overly decorative.

Readings / Watchings

Alongside website references, I collected notes on color systems, visual hierarchy, interface pacing, and the relationship between static composition and interactive movement.

These references informed both the visual tone of the site and the structure of the portfolio as an archive of process.
